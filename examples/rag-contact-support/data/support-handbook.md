# Customer support handbook (demo)

## Warranty

- **Coverage:** **1-year limited warranty** from the delivery date against **manufacturing defects**. Keep your **order ID** and proof of purchase.

- **Not covered:** Accidental damage, normal wear, misuse, or repairs by anyone other than authorized service.

- **How to claim:** Contact support with photos of the issue and your order ID; we may ask you to return the item for inspection.

## Refunds and returns

- **Domestic orders:** Full refund within **30 days** of delivery if the item is unused and in original packaging. Start the return from your account **Orders → Request return**.

- **International orders:** Refunds follow the same **30-day** window where local consumer law allows. Shipping costs for returns may be **non-refundable** unless the product was defective or we shipped the wrong item. Include your **order ID** in every message.

- **Processing time:** Approved refunds appear on the original payment method within **5–10 business days** after we receive the return.

## When to escalate

If the customer is **angry**, mentions **legal action**, or the issue is **outside this handbook**, open a ticket with **`contact_support`** including what you found in the knowledge base and the customer’s goal.

## Contact channels (reference)

- **Live chat:** Not available in this demo environment.
